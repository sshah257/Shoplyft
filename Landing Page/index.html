What's the Biggest Annoyance When Shopping?
Slow Cashiers and Long Lines

65% of consumers agree that the biggest pet peeve when shopping is the excessive wait times at certain stores and at certain times. Following that is the fumbling of cash and tedious credit card logins at the cashier.

Our Solution:
RFID Technology

Radio-frequency identification is an emerging technology that has become very cheap and affordable. By using RFIDs to store each product's information, shopping will become more independant and as simple as self-serve.

How It Works
Part 1

Shopping carts are equipped with two RFID readers. A successful scan from both RFID readers ensure that the product is added to the checkout list when placing the product in the cart. If the product is removed from the cart, the sensor will recogonize this function and take off the item.

How It Works:
Part 2

Every cart has their own unique code that the terminal kiosk will recognize and the associated shopping list will be pulled up. Paying at the kiosk is as easy as tapping your Paypal card and a receipt will be sent to your email VIA SendGrid.
